What is a Visit Visa?

A Visit Visa, also known as a Tourist or Visitor Visa, is issued to individuals travelling abroad for tourism, visiting family or friends, attending events, or short-term personal purposes.

Visit visas are generally temporary and do not permit employment or long-term residence. The duration and entry conditions vary according to the immigration policies of each country.

Typical Documents Required

To ensure a high success rate, you will generally need to prepare the following documentation:

  • Valid Passport
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Completed Visa Application Form
  • Covering Letter stating purpose of visit
  • Confirmed Flight Itinerary
  • Hotel Reservation or Invitation Letter
  • Proof of Sufficient Funds (Bank Statements)
  • Employment or Business Proof
  • Income Tax Returns (if applicable)
  • Travel Insurance (if required)
  • Supporting Documents from Host/Sponsor (if visiting relatives or friends)

What is a Dependent Visa?

A Family or Dependant Visa allows eligible family members such as spouses, children, parents, or other dependants to join their loved ones who are legally residing, studying, or working abroad.

Depending on the immigration regulations of the destination country, dependant visa holders may be permitted to live, study, and in some cases work during the validity of the principal applicant's visa. Family reunification requirements, financial support, and relationship evidence are generally mandatory for approval.

Typical Documents Required

To ensure a high success rate, you will generally need to prepare the following documentation:

  • Valid Passport
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Marriage Certificate (for spouse)
  • Birth Certificate (for children)
  • Proof of Relationship
  • Sponsor's Passport and Visa/Residence Permit Copy
  • Employment or Student Status Documents of Sponsor
  • Financial Proof / Bank Statements
  • Accommodation Proof
  • Medical Insurance (if required)
  • Police Clearance Certificate (if applicable)
  • Completed Visa Application Forms
